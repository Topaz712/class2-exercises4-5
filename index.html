What is a Samurai?

Samurai were members of the japanese warrior caste.

What's so special about Samurai?

The basis of samurai conduct is bushido, "the way of the warrior". This unique philosophy valued honour, reckless bravery and selflessness, as well as duty to the warrior's master with the purpose of giving up one's life and embracing death.

Was there any Female Samurai?

Yes! They were every bit as powerful, smart and deadly as their male counterparts. They were known as the Onna-bugeisha (meaning female martial artist).
